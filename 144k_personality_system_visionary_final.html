Overview

The Visionary represents core traits and roles within the 144K system. The Visionary is driven by a strong sense of purpose and has the ability to see beyond the present moment, allowing them to envision the future. They have a natural ability to inspire others and move them toward a shared goal. The Visionary archetype embodies leadership, creativity, and strategic thinking.
