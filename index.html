The Visionary

The Visionary represents forward-thinking, creativity, and innovation. They have a unique ability to see potential futures and inspire others to follow their vision.

  • The Dream Weaver: Someone who creates and imagines possibilities, often weaving complex ideas into actionable plans.
  • The Innovator: A person who brings new ideas to life, constantly seeking to improve and transform existing systems.
